# 1. PURPOSE

This document defines how CAMera stores, manages, retrieves, promotes, and evolves lessons learned.

The objective is to ensure valuable knowledge is retained and reused across future work.

Lessons transform experience into actionable knowledge.

---

# 2. CORE PHILOSOPHY

Experience should become knowledge.

Knowledge should improve future outcomes.

Knowledge should never be discarded simply because it is old.

History should remain available.

Knowledge should evolve through evidence.

---

# 3. KNOWLEDGE RETENTION POLICY

Lessons are retained permanently.

CAMera does not automatically delete lessons.

Historical knowledge may remain valuable even when no longer actively recommended.

---

# 4. KNOWLEDGE STATUS

Every lesson must have a status.

Possible statuses:

Active

Historical

Superseded

---

# 5. ACTIVE LESSONS

Active lessons are currently recommended.

Characteristics:

- Supported by evidence
- Relevant
- Approved for use

---

# 6. HISTORICAL LESSONS

Historical lessons remain stored for reference.

Characteristics:

- Retained permanently
- Searchable
- Informational

Historical status does not imply invalidity.

---

# 7. SUPERSEDED LESSONS

A lesson may become superseded when a newer lesson provides better guidance.

Superseded lessons remain stored.

They are not deleted.

---

---

---

# 15. MEDIUM CONFIDENCE LESSONS

Characteristics:

- Limited Validation

---

---

# 16. LOW CONFIDENCE LESSONS

Characteristics:

- Limited Evidence
- Few Observations
- Early Findings

---

Low confidence does not imply incorrect.

---

# 17. LOW CONFIDENCE POLICY

Low confidence lessons should not be ignored.

Provide Warning

---
